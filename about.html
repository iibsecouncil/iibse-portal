About IIBSE Council

Indian International Board of Skills School & Education Council (IIBSE) is an autonomous examination and affiliation council established to build a holistic, skill-integrated, and inclusive education system aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023.

IIBSE goes beyond textbooks and examinations by integrating academics, skills, ethics, culture, and real-world readiness for students across urban and rural India.

üë§ Founder

Dr. N. K. Reddy

Founder of IIBSE Council, Dr. N. K. Reddy is a physician, educationist, entrepreneur, and social worker with over two decades of grassroots experience.

He is also the Founder of DKNRSF (NGO) and DKNR Kids School, and has been actively involved in education and social development since the year 2000.

His vision is to create a child-centric, skill-oriented, value-based education system accessible to all, especially rural and small institutions.
