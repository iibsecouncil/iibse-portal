About IIBSE Council

Indian International Board of Skills School & Education Council (IIBSE) is an autonomous examination and affiliation council established with the vision of creating a holistic, skill-integrated, and inclusive education system aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023.

IIBSE addresses the growing need for an education model that goes beyond textbooks and examinations, equally valuing academics, skills, ethics, culture, and real-world readiness.

üë§ Founder

Dr. N. K. Reddy

IIBSE Council was founded by Dr. N. K. Reddy, a professionally qualified physician, educationist, entrepreneur, and social worker.

He is the Founder of DKNRSF (NGO) and DKNR Kids School, and has been actively engaged in social service and educational development since the year 2000.

With decades of grassroots experience, Dr. Reddy envisioned an education system that is child-centric, skill-oriented, value-based, and accessible to all.

üéØ Why IIBSE Council Was Established

  • Over-emphasis on rote learning

IIBSE aims to create a balanced education ecosystem where learning prepares students not only for examinations, but also for life, work, and responsible citizenship.
