Privacy Policy

IIBSE Council

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

IIBSE Council is committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you visit and use the official IIBSE website.

1. Information We Collect

When you access or interact with our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, and contact number
  • Name and details of school, college, or institution
  • Information submitted through enquiry, registration, or contact forms
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device details, and usage data

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to enquiries and requests
  • To provide information about IIBSE Council services and educational support
  • To improve website functionality, content, and user experience
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, or administrative requirements

3. Data Security

IIBSE Council takes appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. However, protecting your information remains our highest priority.

4. Information Sharing

IIBSE Council does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

Information may only be shared:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With the explicit consent of the user

5. Use of Cookies

The website may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website performance. Cookies do not personally identify users but help understand user preferences and improve services.

Users may disable cookies through browser settings if they prefer.

6. Third-Party Links

The IIBSE Council website may contain links to external or third-party websites. IIBSE Council is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

7. Children’s Privacy

IIBSE Council does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If such information is unintentionally collected, suitable steps will be taken to delete it securely.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

IIBSE Council reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of the website signifies acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Information

Email: info@iibse.in
