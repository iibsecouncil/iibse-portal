About IIBSE Affiliation

Indian International Board of Skills School & Education Council (IIBSE) offers affiliation to private unaided schools, colleges, institutions, and training centers committed to delivering quality, inclusive, and future-ready education.

The IIBSE affiliation framework supports institutions in implementing skill-integrated, value-based, and learner-centric education aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023.

üéØ Purpose of IIBSE Affiliation

  • Promote holistic education balancing academics and skills
  • Support foundational and early childhood education
  • Enable rural and small schools through flexible norms
  • Encourage innovation, values, and real-world learning
  • Ensure quality, transparency, and accountability

Affiliation with IIBSE is a partnership for educational growth and continuous improvement.

üßí Foundational & Kids School Affiliation

IIBSE places special emphasis on Foundational and Kids Schools, recognizing early education as the most critical stage of learning.

Suitable for:

  • Nursery, Pre-Primary, LKG, UKG
  • Early Primary Schools
  • Kids Schools in rural, semi-urban, and urban areas

Key Features:

  • Play-based and activity-oriented learning
  • Focus on language, numeracy, culture, and values
  • Age-appropriate early skill exposure
  • Flexible and supportive affiliation norms
