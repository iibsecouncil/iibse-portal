Overview

The IIBSE Curriculum is designed to provide a balanced, holistic, and future-ready learning experience, integrating academic knowledge, practical skills, values, and life competencies. It is aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 and National Curriculum Framework (NCF) 2023, ensuring relevance, flexibility, and inclusivity for learners across India.

IIBSE believes that education must prepare students not only for examinations, but for real life, careers, and responsible citizenship.

üßí Strong Focus on Foundational Education

IIBSE places special emphasis on Foundational and Early Childhood Education, recognizing it as the most critical stage of learning.

  • Play-based and activity-based learning
  • Language and numeracy development
  • Cultural awareness and moral values
  • Early exposure to practical and life skills

This foundation supports nursery, pre-primary, and early-grade schools, especially rural and small institutions, helping children build strong learning habits from the beginning.
