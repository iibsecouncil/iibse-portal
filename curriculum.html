Curriculum Overview

The IIBSE Curriculum is designed to deliver a balanced, holistic, and future-ready learning experience that integrates academics, practical skills, values, and life competencies.

Aligned with NEP 2020 and NCF 2023, it ensures relevance, flexibility, and inclusivity for learners across urban, rural, and underserved regions of India.
