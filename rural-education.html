Rural Education at IIBSE

The IIBSE Rural Education Initiative is a dedicated effort to strengthen and support village, rural, and small schools, ensuring that quality education reaches every child, regardless of geography, infrastructure, or economic background.

IIBSE believes that rural education is the foundation of national development. By empowering rural schools with flexible systems, skill-integrated learning, and value-based education, IIBSE works to create equal learning opportunities.
