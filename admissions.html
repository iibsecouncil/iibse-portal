Admissions Framework at IIBSE

The IIBSE Council Admissions Framework is designed to ensure a simple, transparent, and inclusive admission process for students enrolling under IIBSE-affiliated schools, institutions, and programs.

IIBSE believes that admissions should be accessible, fair, and learner-focused, supporting students from diverse social, economic, and geographic backgrounds, including rural and semi-urban areas.
