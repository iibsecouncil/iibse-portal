DKNRSF (Founding Organization)

Dr. Krishna Ninga Reddy Social Foundation (DKNRSF) is the founding organization behind the establishment of IIBSE Council.

DKNRSF has played a conceptual and advisory role in shaping IIBSE as a skill-integrated, institution-friendly education council.

IIBSE Council operates independently as an autonomous educational body.
